Soondubu Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 green onion chopped, divided
  • 1/4 onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tbsp Gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)
  • 1 tbsp each soy sauce, hoisin sauce, or oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 cups water anchovy stock, dashi, or unsalted chicken stock
  • 1/4 zucchini diced
  • 60g mushrooms
  • 1 package soft tofu
  • 1 egg
  • 1 chili pepper chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil and sesame oil in a pot over medium heat. Add white parts of green onion and onion and cook until fragrant. Add garlic, gochugaru, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar and mix to combine.
  2. Add water or stock. Once it comes to a boil, add zucchini, mushrooms, and soft tofu. Using a spoon, slice the tofu into large pieces.
  3. Bring to a boil and simmer until the flavors have melded, for about 3-4 minutes. Season with salt to taste, crack an egg, and garnish with green onion and chili pepper if using. Serve with a side of rice and enjoy!
